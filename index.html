The Next Step for Household Solar Power









Solar panels are not efficient

Since not all houses are the same, solar panels provide variable returns to homeowners because solar panels are fixed. Currently there exists no way to move residential solar panels based on the position of the sun.





Our Solution: A solar panel that rotates based on the angle of the sun.

We aim to solve this problem by designing something that can adjust solar panels for homeowners. The details are, as of now, lacking, but hopefully by the time we actually put up this website I can delete this part.






How It Works


The solar panel has sensors that pick up how much light it is receiving. The sensors then tell the panel which direction will provide the most sunlight and command an engine to move the panel to the optimal height. This maximizes energy collected by the sun and decreases the payback period of the solar panels.
