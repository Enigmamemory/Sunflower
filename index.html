Solar panels have variable returns for homeowners. One reason for this is due to the position of the sun relative to the panels. But solar panels made for houses are often slanted and currently there is no technology that allows them to adjust based on the position of the sun.



We aim to solve this problem by designing something that can adjust solar panels for homeowners. The details are, as of now, lacking, but hopefully by the time we actually put up this website I can delete this part.
